Our Mission

Are you ready to join the fight against Shinra? Are you tired of standing idly by while the planet is plundered for its resources?
Then Avalanche wants you!
We are a passionate group of freedom fighters dedicated to protecting the planet and striking back against the corrupt Shinra Electric Power Company.
Our mission is to put an end to their destructive ways and preserve the world for future generations.
As a member of Avalanche, you'll work alongside some of the most talented and dedicated fighters in the world. You'll embark on dangerous missions, hone your combat skills, and make a real difference in the fight for a better future.
Join us and be a part of something bigger than yourself.

Join Avalanche today!

Our Members

Barret Wallace

A passionate and determined leader, Barret is the founder of Avalanche and a skilled fighter with a machine gun arm. He is fiercely dedicated to the cause of protecting the planet from Shinra's exploitation and will stop at nothing to achieve his goals.

Tifa Lockhart

A talented martial artist and the proprietor of Seventh Heaven Bar, Tifa is a loyal friend and a valued member of Avalanche. She is known for her quick reflexes, impressive strength, and unwavering dedication to the cause.

Biggs

One of the key members of Avalanche's tactical team, Biggs is a skilled engineer and a trusted ally to Barret. He is responsible for designing and building many of the team's weapons and gadgets, and his technical expertise is a valuable asset in the fight against Shinra.

Wedge

A gifted hacker and tech expert, Wedge is an essential member of Avalanche's intelligence-gathering team. He is responsible for infiltrating Shinra's systems and gathering information that the team needs to plan their missions.

Jessie Raspberry

Jessie is Avalanche’s go-to tech whiz: hacking, faking IDs―you name it, she does it!
A clever and resourceful member of Avalanche, Jessie is a master of explosives and a key player in many of the team's plans.
She is an expert in demolitions and is responsible for creating and executing many of the team's most daring operations.

Our Base

Welcome to Sector 7, the bustling and vibrant neighborhood nestled in the heart of Midgar!
Looking for excitement and adventure? Look no further than Sector 7! With its lively bars, bustling streets, and diverse population, Sector 7 is the place to be for those who crave the thrill of urban living.
And don't forget about the Sector 7 slums, where the hard-working and resilient residents make their homes. These industrious folk keep the city running, and their spirit is an inspiration to all.
So come on down to Sector 7, where the lights are bright, the music is loud, and the adventure never stops!

As the sun sets over Midgar, the neon glow of Seventh Heaven Bar beckons you in.
Step inside and immerse yourself in the lively atmosphere of this iconic hangout.
Savor the delicious aromas of food and drink, and soak up the sounds of music and laughter.

Take a seat at the bar and enjoy a frothy beer, or indulge in a plate of steaming hot food. With its warm, inviting ambiance and friendly patrons, Seventh Heaven Bar is the perfect place to unwind and let your worries fade away. Come and experience the magic of this legendary bar for yourself.

Meet Tifa Lockhart, the heart and soul of Seventh Heaven Bar.
As the proprietor of this legendary establishment, Tifa is known for her warm and welcoming demeanor, her exceptional bartending skills, and her fierce loyalty to her friends and customers.
With her striking beauty and no-nonsense attitude, Tifa commands respect and admiration from all who know her.
Whether you're looking for a cold drink, a hot meal, or just some friendly conversation, Tifa is always happy to oblige.
Stop by Seventh Heaven Bar and experience the magic of Tifa's hospitality for yourself!
